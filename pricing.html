What's Included

Everything your OSP needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built specifically for the grant lifecycle at Historically Black Colleges and Universities and land-grant institutions.

📋
NOI Wizard
6-step guided submission form. Researchers complete a Notice of Intent in under 10 minutes. Saves directly to Firestore with full compliance flags.
📊
12-Stage Pipeline Tracker
Kanban board and table view. OSP advances proposals stage-by-stage. Every move is timestamped with a full history log. PIs see their status in real time.
🤖
AI Grant Assistant
Gemini-powered chat with voice. Knows your institution's rules, deadlines, IDC rates, and approval chain. Answers PI questions 24/7 so your staff doesn't have to.
📋
IRB Protocol Draft Generator
When a proposal flags human subjects research, Grant AI auto-generates a complete 13-section IRB protocol outline. Copy, export to PDF, or regenerate.
🔬
Research Match
Upload an abstract or PDF. Gemini reads it, identifies research themes, and matches it against active funding opportunities — then pre-fills the NOI form.
⏰
Automated Deadline Reminders
Daily scheduled function sends 14-day and 7-day email reminders to PIs before sponsor deadlines. OSP gets a 7-day admin alert. Never miss a deadline.
👤
PI Self-Service Portal
Researchers sign in with Google, see only their proposals, track progress through the pipeline, read OSP notes, and export their own NOI as a branded PDF.
📄
Branded PDF Export
One-click PDF export for every NOI — institution colors, OSP contact info, all fields. Runs entirely in the browser. No server, no queue, instant.
📧
Transactional Email Notifications
PIs get a confirmation email on submission and an update every time their proposal advances a stage. Fully branded HTML emails via Resend.
Deployment

Live at your college
in 30 minutes.

The entire system is designed to be redeployed at any university by swapping one configuration file.

1
Fork the repository
One GitHub fork. No code changes yet — everything institution-specific lives in a single config file.
2 minutes
2
Edit institution-config.js
Institution name, college, brand colors, logo, assistant name, OSP contact info, and admin email. That's it.
5 minutes
3
Create Firebase project
Enable Firestore and Google Auth. Copy the config into firebase.js. Set 3 service account env vars for deadline reminders.
10 minutes
4
Deploy to Netlify
Connect the GitHub repo to a new Netlify site. Set 7 environment variables (API keys). Add the Netlify domain to Firebase Auth.
10 minutes
✓
Push — it's live.
Auto-deploy takes ~60 seconds. Your college has a fully working, AI-powered grant management system.
